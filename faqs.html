Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

Browse books on our shop page, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your details and choose a payment method (COD or Razorpay).

2. Do I need an account to order?

Yes, you need to sign in or create an account to place orders or submit bulk order requests.

3. What payment methods are available?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) and online payments via Razorpay (cards, UPI, net banking).

4. How long does shipping take?

Orders are delivered within 5-7 business days in India. Check our shipping policy for details.

5. Can I return a book?

Yes, books can be returned within 10 days if unused and in original condition. See our terms and conditions for the return process.

6. How do I place a bulk order?

Use the bulk order form in the navbar or footer. Provide details like book titles, quantities, and delivery address.

7. How can I track my order?

You’ll receive a tracking link via email once your order is dispatched.

8. How do I contact customer support?
